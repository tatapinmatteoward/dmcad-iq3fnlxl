## What is a DMCA Takedown?

The DMCA allows copyright holders to request the removal of allegedly infringing material from online platforms. ## Common Pitfalls for Creators

Many creators receive DMCA notices due to fair use misunderstandings or automated false positives. Don't panic! Instead:

- **Review the Claim**: Check if the content truly infringes. Fair use protections might apply for commentary, criticism, or parody.
- **Gather Evidence**: Document your original creation process to counter claims.
- **Respond Promptly**: Platforms like YouTube or WordPress require quick action to avoid permanent removal.
